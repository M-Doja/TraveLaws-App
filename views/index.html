TravState is your one stop resource for law information on different U.S. states. Whether you're on vacation or you're looking to move your business to another state, TravState is the resource for you!

What is TravState?

TravState is a resource used to find different state laws for different states all on one site. As some U.S. states don't have goverment websites regarding certain areas of law, here on TravState we make it easy for you to find and connect to those corresponding online state resources.

What is TravState?

The idea behind TravState is that if you are travelling or need information on another state that you are not familiar with, you can use this app to get high level overview of the laws, as well as a few basic demographics of the state you are interested in.

Currently this app is still under development even though is is live. Please do continue to check back with us as we continue to improve and add more features.
